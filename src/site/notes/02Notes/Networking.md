# What is Networking?

**Networking** is the practice of connecting computers and other devices so they can share data, resources, and services.

Networking refers to how computers communicate

## Terminology

- **Devices**: Routers, servers, switches etc.
- **Connections**: Wired (ethernet, fiber) or wireless (Wi-Fi, Bluetooth)
- **[[02Notes/Application Protocol\|Protocols]]**: rules for communication like **TCP/IP**, **HTTP**, or **DNS**.
